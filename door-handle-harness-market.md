<p><p>The door handle harness market consists of two main types: In-Door Wiring Harness and Under The Door Harness. The In-Door Wiring Harness is integrated within the door assembly, providing connectivity for electrical components like power locks, windows, and lighting. In contrast, the Under The Door Harness is designed to run beneath the door, facilitating connections for sensors and other external components. Both types are essential for enhancing vehicle functionality, improving safety features, and ensuring seamless operation of door-related systems.</p></p>

<p><p>The ceramic ink market comprises two main types: Functional Ink and Normal Ink. Normal Ink is standard, primarily used for decorative purposes like printing designs on tiles or tableware. Both types are integral to the ceramic industry, with Functional Ink catering to technical needs and Normal Ink focusing on aesthetic and traditional applications.</p></p>

<p><strong>The Resistant Potato Starch Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>RS2</li><li>RS3</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The resistant potato starch market is primarily categorized into two types: RS2 and RS3. RS2 refers to native resistant starch found in raw potatoes, which is less digestible and beneficial for gut health. RS3, on the other hand, is formed when cooked and cooled potatoes undergo retrogradation, making it more accessible and functional in food applications. Both types are gaining popularity as gluten-free and low-calorie ingredients in various food products, driven by rising health consciousness among consumers.</p></p>

<p><ul><li>Cable Driver</li><li>Chain Driver</li><li>Traction (MRL) Elevator</li><li>Hydraulic Elevator</li><li>Pneumatic Lift</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The home elevators market is categorized into several types: </p><p>1. Cable-driven elevators utilize a system of cables and pulleys, offering smooth and quiet operation.</p><p>2. Chain-driven elevators employ a chain mechanism for lifting, known for durability and low maintenance.</p><p>3. Traction (MRL) elevators, or machine-room-less elevators, use electric motors to lift the cab, saving space.</p><p>4. Hydraulic elevators depend on fluid-driven pistons for vertical movement, suitable for low-rise applications.</p><p>5. Pneumatic lifts operate using air pressure, ideal for compact spaces and ease of installation.</p></p>

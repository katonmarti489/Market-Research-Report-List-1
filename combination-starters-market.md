<p><p>Combination starters are essential for controlling electric motors and come in several market types. Full-voltage non-reversing starters provide direct power without reverse operation, ideal for simple applications. Full-voltage reversing starters allow for forward and reverse motor control, enhancing operational flexibility. Multi-speed starters enable varying motor speeds for precise control in different applications. Reduced voltage starters limit initial power to reduce inrush current, protecting the motor and associated equipment during startup. Each type serves specific operational needs in industrial settings.</p></p>

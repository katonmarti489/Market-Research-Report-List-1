<p><ul><li>Centrifugal Pumps</li><li>Displacement Pumps</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The agriculture pump set market primarily features two types: centrifugal pumps and displacement pumps. Centrifugal pumps utilize rotational energy from a motor to move water, making them efficient for high-flow applications like irrigation and drainage. Displacement pumps, on the other hand, operate by trapping a fixed volume of fluid and forcing it out, suitable for tasks requiring high pressure, such as well dewatering. Both types cater to diverse agricultural needs, optimizing water management and enhancing productivity.</p></p>

<p><p>The Automotive Air-conditioning System market includes various types, such as the CCOT (Cycling Clutch Orifice Tube) System, TXV (Thermal Expansion Valve) System, and others. The CCOT system uses an orifice tube to regulate refrigerant flow, cycling the compressor clutch to maintain temperature. The TXV system employs a thermal expansion valve for precise refrigerant control, ensuring consistent cooling. Other systems may incorporate advanced technologies like variable displacement compressors or hybrid solutions, catering to diverse vehicle requirements and enhancing energy efficiency, comfort, and performance.</p></p>

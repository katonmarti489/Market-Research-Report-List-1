<p><p>Bearing remanufacturing involves the refurbishment of various types of bearings to extend their lifespan and enhance performance. Plain bearings, featuring smooth surfaces for load support, are common in low-friction applications. Rolling-element bearings utilize balls or rollers to reduce friction, making them ideal for high-speed operations. Magnetic bearings employ magnetic fields to levitate the rotor, minimizing contact and wear. Other types may include specialized or composite bearings tailored for unique industrial needs, contributing to sustainability by reducing waste.</p></p>

<p><p>The commercial sweeping machine market is primarily divided into two types: ride-on sweepers and walk-behind/pedestrian sweepers. Ride-on sweepers are designed for larger areas, offering greater efficiency and speed as the operator sits on the machine, ideal for parking lots and large warehouses. In contrast, walk-behind or pedestrian sweepers are compact, allowing for maneuverability in tighter spaces, suitable for sidewalks and small outdoor areas. Both types are essential for maintaining cleanliness in various commercial environments.</p></p>

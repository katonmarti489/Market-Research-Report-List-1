<p><p>The Electric Vehicle Motor Control Unit (MCU) is a critical component in electric vehicles (EVs) that manages the operation of the electric motor. The market is expected to grow at a compound annual growth rate (CAGR) of 14.8% during the forecast period. As the EV industry evolves, the MCU market is poised to play a pivotal role in shaping the future of sustainable transportation.</p></p>

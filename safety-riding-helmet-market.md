<p><p>The safety riding helmet market is categorized into three main types: full face helmets, open face helmets, and half helmets. Full face helmets offer maximum protection, covering the entire head and face, ideal for high-speed riding. Open face helmets provide good visibility and ventilation while safeguarding the sides and back of the head. Half helmets, being the least protective, cover only the top of the head, prioritizing comfort and style for casual riders. Each type caters to varying rider preferences and safety needs.</p></p>

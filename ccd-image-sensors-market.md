<p><strong>The CCD Image Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Frame Transfer CCD Sensor</li><li>Full Frame Transfer CCD Sensor</li><li>Interline Transfer CCD Sensor</li><li>Frame Interline Transfer CCD Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The CCD image sensors market includes various types categorized by their structure and functionality. Frame Transfer CCD sensors offer rapid readout by transferring charge to a storage area, enhancing efficiency. Full Frame Transfer CCD sensors capture images in their entirety before reading, suitable for applications needing high resolution. Interline Transfer CCD sensors utilize alternating rows for simultaneous exposure and readout, minimizing blurring. Frame Interline Transfer CCD sensors combine features of both, providing flexibility in imaging while retaining speed, making them ideal for diverse applications.</p></p>

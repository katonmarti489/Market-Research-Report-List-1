Additionally, the rise in infrastructure development and urbanization in emerging economies is fueling market expansion.</p><p>Recent trends in the Ethylene Norbornene Market include a shift toward sustainable and bio-based materials, as industries aim to reduce their environmental footprint. Furthermore, the growing adoption of electric vehicles (EVs) is creating new opportunities for ENB-based elastomers, as they are essential for manufacturing durable and heat-resistant components.

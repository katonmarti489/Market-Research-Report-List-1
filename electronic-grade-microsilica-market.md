<p><p>The Electronic Grade Microsilica market is primarily divided into angular and spherical types, each serving distinct purposes. Angular microsilica, characterized by its jagged edges, enhances the mechanical properties and resistance of composite materials, making it suitable for applications in electronics and construction. In contrast, spherical microsilica has a smooth, round shape that improves flowability and packing density, ideal for use in electronic components and coatings. Both types play critical roles in enhancing performance across various industries.</p></p>

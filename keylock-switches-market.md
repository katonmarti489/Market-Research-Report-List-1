<p><p>Keylock switches are essential components in access control systems, offering secure operation for various applications. Single-pole keylock switches control one circuit, allowing for straightforward on/off functionality, typically used in less complex devices. In contrast, double-pole keylock switches manage two circuits simultaneously, providing more complexity and versatility, suitable for high-power applications or where independent control of two circuits is necessary. The market for these switches continues to grow as industries seek enhanced security and control solutions.</p></p>

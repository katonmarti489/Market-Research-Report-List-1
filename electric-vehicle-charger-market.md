<p><p>The Electric Vehicle (EV) charger market is divided into **on-board** and **off-board** chargers. **On-board chargers** are integrated into the vehicle, converting AC power from charging stations to DC for the battery. They are compact and convenient for home or slow public charging. **Off-board chargers** are external, high-power units, often used in fast or ultra-fast charging stations, delivering DC power directly to the battery. On-board chargers suit daily use, while off-board chargers cater to rapid charging needs, enhancing EV adoption and convenience.</p></p>

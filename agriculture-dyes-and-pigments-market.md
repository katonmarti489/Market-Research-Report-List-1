<p><p>The agriculture dyes and pigments market comprises two main types: dyes and pigments. Pigments, on the other hand, are insoluble colorants that provide long-lasting color and opacity, commonly utilized in coatings and fertilizers. Both play a crucial role in enhancing the appearance and functionality of agricultural products, while also contributing to branding and product differentiation.</p></p>

Antioxidants prevent the oxidation of hydraulic fluids, enhancing their longevity and performance. Anti-emulsifiers facilitate the separation of water and oil, preventing emulsification that can lead to equipment damage. Rust inhibitors protect metallic components from corrosion and rust formation, thereby extending the lifespan of machinery. Together, these additives ensure efficient operation, reliability, and reduced maintenance costs in hydraulic systems.</p></p>

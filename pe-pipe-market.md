<p><p>The PE pipe market consists of three main types: HDPE, MDPE, and LDPE. HDPE pipes are known for their high strength and durability, making them ideal for water and gas distribution. MDPE pipes offer a balance of strength and flexibility, suitable for medium-pressure applications. LDPE pipes are more flexible and lightweight, often used in irrigation and lower-pressure systems. Each type caters to specific applications based on factors like pressure requirements, environmental conditions, and installation needs.</p></p>

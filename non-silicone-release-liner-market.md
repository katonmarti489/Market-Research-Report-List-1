<p><p>The non-silicone release liner market comprises two primary types: release liner paper and release liner film. Release liner paper is typically made from thick sheets of paper coated with a release agent, providing a suitable substrate for various adhesive applications. In contrast, release liner film is made from polymer materials, offering enhanced durability and moisture resistance. Both types serve as protective layers for adhesives and labels, facilitating easy peeling and application in industries such as packaging, automotive, and electronics.</p></p>
